## Path Grounding Rules

- Do not create `src/ppi/analysis` for this migration unless the inventory first records a deliberate repository reorganization away from `src/ppi/core`.
- Do not create `frontend/src/features` or `frontend/src/lib` for this migration unless the inventory first records a deliberate frontend reorganization away from `frontend/src/api`, `frontend/src/transforms`, `frontend/src/pages`, and `frontend/src/components`.
- A pipeline cannot move from `not-started` to `partially-migrated` until its exact current entrypoint files and target path family are filled in.
- A compatibility adapter cannot move to `migrated`; it must be removed, replaced by real stages, or explicitly deferred with a cleanup task.
